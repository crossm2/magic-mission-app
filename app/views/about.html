How To Use This App

Imagine this app as a box of notecards. Each notecard has 10 exercises, so when you go to the gym, you can pick a notecard and have a great workout.

It's customizable, so you can skip exercises you don't like and the app will suggest a new one instead. Each exercise has instructions so you can learn proper form.
